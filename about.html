so what's with the personal site anyway?

easy! here are several reasons why, kept simple:


  • 1. i'm sick and tired of the state of social media in the internet
  • 2. i hate how in such sites, you're not able to express yourself anymore, and what you can do is marginal at best
  • 3. i absolutely adore the old web and wanted to add to it with my own little site
  • 4. having something like this feels more permanent compared to a social media account that can be taken down at any time
  • 5. this acts as a different kind of creative outlet for me personally!
  • 6. also it's really cool to have your own domain. cooler than getting your own username in a social media website bc you can do so many things with a domain! like hey look at this cool thing i've built from the ground up!!
