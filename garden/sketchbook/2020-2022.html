i have to acknowledge here that most of the art here has a lot of heavy weight. i will not elaborate as to why. just know that while i have completely separated myself from relevant subjects, as i have not been involved in such spaces for years, this is the time period where i also had the most growth with my art. the community i was in was volatile, but the people i had surrounded myself there in my own little circle were all the most talented artists that inspired me every day, and they were the reason why i stayed in the community for as long as i did.

also, please take care of yourself, artist or not. the numbers are never worth it compared to your personal health.
